Manage Stress Without Alcohol

Practice deep breathing:
When stressed, take a moment to breathe deeply. Try the 4-7-8 technique:
Inhale for 4 seconds, hold your breath for 7 seconds, and exhale slowly for 8 seconds. Repeat three times. Use physical activity:
Exercise is a proven stress reliever. Even a 10-minute walk can help. Try setting an alarm for every two hours to stand up and stretch or do a quick walk around your house or office. Create a relaxation routine:
At the end of the day, instead of reaching for a drink, set aside 20 minutes for yourself. Listen to calming music, do light stretches, or take a warm bath to relax.
