Find Healthier Alternatives

Experiment with mocktails:
Try mixing sparkling water, fruit juice, and herbs (like mint or basil) to create flavorful, non-alcoholic drinks.
A simple recipe could be:
Sparkling water, a splash of lime juice, and a few cucumber slices for a refreshing mocktail.
Non-alcoholic alternatives:
Keep soda, flavored water, or herbal teas available. When you feel the urge to drink, grab one of these alternatives instead.
Swap activities:
If you tend to drink during certain activities (like watching TV), swap that activity for a new hobby. For example, take up evening walks, start a home workout routine, or read a book in place of drinking.
