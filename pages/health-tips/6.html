Celebrate Small Wins

Set small, frequent rewards:
Each week you achieve your goal (like reducing the number of drinks or having a sober day), reward yourself. The reward could be a small treat, like your favorite dessert, a movie, or something fun.
Create a reward jar:
Every time you avoid alcohol when tempted, put a small note or even some money into a jar. Over time, this will become a visual reminder of your success. Use the jar’s contents to buy something you’ve been wanting.
Keep a progress journal:
Every night, write down one positive change you noticed that day. Whether it's improved sleep or better focus, celebrating these benefits can reinforce your commitment.
